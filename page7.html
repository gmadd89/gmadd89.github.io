About This Project

While currently based in Kentucky, I was originally born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. When tasked to make a hillshade of a geograpically interesting area, I chose to map an interesting feature from my home state. For this map, I first made a Digital Elevation Model, or DEM, and then used that DEM in blender to make a hillshade.
