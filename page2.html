About This Project

As noted in my bio, I have strong military ties. When tasked with creating a narrative map that tells a visual story, I chose to focus on a battle that is well known in my squadron. This map uses a hillshade and visual storytelling elements to highlight one of the deadliest battles in the operation, as seven servicemen lost their lives.
