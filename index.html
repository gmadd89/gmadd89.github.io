About Me

I am an upperclassman at the University of Kentucky. While I am majoring in Geography, I have taken a number of courses in International Relations. I am also a Tech Sergeant in the Kentucky Air National Guard and was active-duty Air Force before my guard service. During my nine years in the military, I have worked mainly in geospatial imagery and intelligence. Through my military service and educational background, I have developed a wide array of tools to develop diverse maps. See my work here and contact me if you would like help on a project in the future!
